# The Eternity Mod
The Eternity Mod is a Terraria Mod that focuses on adding new content to spice up your Terraria experience as well as adding some Quality of Life changes. The Eternity Mod adds tons of new bosses, minibosses, town npcs, enemies, biomes, structures, and a new gamemode, Nightmare Mode. It also adds tons of Items such as materials, furnitures, weapons, armors, tools, hooks, mounts, pets, accessories and so mucn more. It will add hours of more content for you to discover and progression stretches out through the entire game including prehardmode, hardmode and post-moon lord. The Eternity Mod will also feature its own lore and story. The mod also has cross-over content with other mods and is compatible with most mods.
